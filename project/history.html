Premier League Triumph

In the 2019-2020 season, Liverpool FC won their first Premier League title in 30 years. Under the management of Jürgen Klopp, the team dominated the league, finishing with 99 points.

Champions League Glory

In 2019, Liverpool claimed their sixth UEFA Champions League title by defeating Tottenham Hotspur 2-0 in the final. This victory solidified their status as one of Europe's elite clubs.

FA Cup and League Cup Double

In the 2021-2022 season, Liverpool achieved a domestic cup double, winning both the FA Cup and the League Cup. Both finals were decided on penalties against Chelsea.
