Ivory Coast

Ivory Coast, also known as Côte d'Ivoire, is a country located in West Africa. It is known for its diverse culture, rich history, and vibrant economy. The country is the world's largest producer of cocoa beans and has a significant agricultural sector. Ivory Coast is also home to beautiful landscapes, including beaches, rainforests, and national parks. The capital city is Yamoussoukro, while the largest city and economic hub is Abidjan.
